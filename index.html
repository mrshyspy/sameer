About the Advocate

Sameer Khan

B.A. LL.B.  |  Advocate, Rajasthan Bar

Sameer Khan is a dedicated legal professional committed to providing practical legal solutions and strong representation. With a client-focused approach, he assists individuals and businesses in navigating legal matters with confidence and clarity.

Practicing in Ramgarh, Alwar, Rajasthan, Sameer Khan brings thorough knowledge of civil, criminal, and family law. He is known for meticulous attention to detail, transparent communication, and unwavering commitment to protecting his clients' rights and interests at every step of the legal process.
